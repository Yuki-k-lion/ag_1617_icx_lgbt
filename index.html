Our Concept

Why we approach this topic.

In the future, the diversity of people will further accelerate.

Aging of the society, disparity in wealth, and LGBT.

Now, this world needs a society where diverse people of various ages, sexes, nationality, individuality can coexist.

We want to solve problems arise in a changing the world and create a society that can maximize the possibilities of all people.

In order to achieve that, we believe young people themselves need to promote diversity.

This program aims to promote Japan's diversity with you and various companies and organizations of society.

Time Line

This is what you will do in this program.

  • Pre-Activity

    #1

    Upbringing of the next-generation LGBT leader Event design with LITALICO Inc., marketing to press-agent an event, hearing from young LGBT leader

    #2

    Thinking about Diversity with high school students Ask cooperate for the high school through the intermediation of the Shibuya-ku government office. (before visit to Japan) Class design with LITALICO. Learning about the LGBT education to high school students from Rebit

    #3

    Making community for sharing a trouble Ask LGBT people and LGBT people having babies for their opinions Learn about the online media and the online community production.

  • During Internship

    #1

    Having discussions to solve the problem of LGBT people.(with LITALICO Inc, people in Diversity section of Shibuya-ku government office, AIESEC Member and other NPO.)

    #2

    Making a special class in a high school in Shibuya. Explain "Diversity"and introduce action of Shibuya-ku and LITALICO Inc. Make a workshop thinking about Diversity with high school students.

    #3

    Create the place for parents having LGBT children to share their troubles. Through this activity, we want to add LGBT topic in "a development navigator" (one of LITALICO’s servises) in future.

  • After Activity

    #1

    Suggest the output by the event to LITALICO Inc. and the Shibuya-ku government office. Grope for how we can make use in future action together.

    #2

    Shoot the action with a video camera and deliver it through the Internet. Report the class for Shibuya-ku to use it for a future action.

    #3

    Write some information in HP of Shibuya-ku and the school and. Make a class in cooperation with Shibuya University(a NPO in Shibuya) to spread information to the generation of the parent.
